Isle 3

Main Boses of Isle 3:

Rumor Honeybottoms:

Rumor Honeybottoms is the first level you come across when entering isle 3. Isle 3 is considerably harder than isle 1 & 2. This level shows how difficult the jump is, requiring a lot of concentration to complete. You battle inside a beehive, as honey rises all around you. You must defeat the queen bee over three phases.

Captain Brineybeard:

Captain Brineybeard is the second boss from isle 3. Located along the beach, you battle this boss as he sits ontop of his ship attached to the pier. This level is one of the more achievable levels on isle 3. Its strongly advised to attach the green chaser shots for this mission so you can easily reach Captain Brineybeard.

Sally Stageplay:

One of the calmer levels from isle 3. Sally stageplay is considerably boring compared to the rest of her counterparts. The level is set inside a theatre. There are four phases with the final phase being the easiest to complete, so if you make it to phases 4. You know you have done the hard work.

Werner Werman

Werner Werman is a german rat who slightly depicts the cartoon mouse Jerry from Tom and Jerry. This level is very close quarters. Its is strongly advised to equip the red triple shot to deal maximum damage.

Dr Khal's Robot:

Dr Khal's Robot is the penultimate flying level before finishing isle 3. This level is extremely difficult to complete as there are multiple attacks on the player simultaniously. It's best to use the small plane as much as possible to dodge the varying attacks./p>

Cala Maria:

Cala Maria is a medusa like villain in the game. This boss has the ability to freeze the player in stone while attacking simultaniously. Leaving the player stranded in the path of attack. You can shake yourself out of the freeze if hit by the bosses gaze.

Phantom Express:

The Phantom Express is the final boss of isle 3. This level is a well rounded level with 4 phases containing a different boss within each phase.
