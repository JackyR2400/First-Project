Isle 2

Main Boses of Isle 2:

Baroness Von Bon Bon:

Baroness Von Bon Bon is the first boss of the isle 2. This level has four phases, with the final phase being the most difficult and longest in length. It is advised to have green chaser rounds attached when trying to fight this boss.

Djimmi the Great:

Djimmi the Great is the second level which uses flight. There are 5 phases to this level. Phases 5 is by far the most difficult of the level. A good piece of advise is to save up your cards until you can use your nuke ultimate.

Beppi the Clown:

Beppi the Clown is the third level on isle 2. A fast past level which entails a clown boss riding on various attractions from a theme park. There are four phases to this level. The last phase has small baseball villains which are the most likely to end your attempt at this level.

Wally Warbles:

Wally Warbles is the third flying boss of the game. Your enemy in this level is a bird that has very strong attack powers. It is useful to transform regularly to the smaller plane to avoid the bosses regular attacks.

Grim Matchstick:

Grim Matchstick is by far the most difficult boss to fight on isle 2. The level has three phases with the final stage being extremely fast past and dangerous. Most of the screen will be attacked by a fire stream aiming at the player. It is best to stay nice and high on this level as you can dash to nearby platforms easily.
